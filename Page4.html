The Space Race

    Sputnik 1 Launch
  • The Soviet Union launched Sputnik 1, the first artificial satelite, marking the beginning of the space race
      • Yuri Gagarin's Flight
  • Soviet cosomnaut Yuri Gagarin became the first human to orbit the Earth aboard Vostok 1, a major milestone in space exploration.
      • Apollo 11 Moon Landing
  • NASA's Apollo 11 mission successfully landed humans on the Moon, with Neil Armstrong becoming the first person to walk on its surface
      • Explorer 1 Launch
  • The United States launced its first satellite, Explorer 1, marking its entery into the space race and leading to the discovery of the Van Allen radiation belts.
      • Luna 2 Impact
  • The Soviet Union's Luna 2 became the first spacecraft to reach the Moon, crashing onto its surface and proving direct lunar impact was possible
    • "One small step for man, one giant leap for mankind"

    -Neil Armstrong
